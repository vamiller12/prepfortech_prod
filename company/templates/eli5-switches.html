TL;DR - Switches

In short, switches are hardware devices that act as a switching point signals between two or more otherwise unconnected devices. Since data transfer via the internet has limitations on transfer speed, devices within a data center are often connected via ethernet cables. To connect the servers without having to increase the number of ports on each server, switches act as the central hub.
