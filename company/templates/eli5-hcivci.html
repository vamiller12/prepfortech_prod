TL;DR - Hyperconverged Infrastructure vs Converged Infrastructure

HCI is virtualizing the already combined compute, storage, and networking functions, while converged infrastructure is combining the same compute, storage, and networking functions but without the virtualization option. The creation of converged infrastructure was a game changer based on the ability to simplify the management of a data center by being a turn-key application. Hyperconverged infrastructure was the next iteration of data center efficiency by employing the virtualization concepts which create pools of memory and compute power.
